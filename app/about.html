ABOUT

Scrumble is a Chattanooga-first daily vote on local culture, food, neighborhoods, and nightlife.

We keep it simple: two options, one choice, quick results.

Vote integrity: votes are rate-limited and monitored for suspicious patterns to keep results useful and fair.

Editorial style: curated local matchups, not generic directory listings.

Questions, press, or partnerships? Email hello@scrumble.cc.
